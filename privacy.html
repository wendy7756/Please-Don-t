Privacy Policy

We take your privacy seriously. "Please Don't" is a minimalist app and does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

We do NOT:

  • Require user accounts
  • Collect analytics or device identifiers
  • Store your input text or usage data
  • Use third-party trackers or SDKs

What we do:

  • Use local device speech synthesis (AVSpeechSynthesizer) when text-to-speech is enabled.
  • Store your preferences (like selected colors) locally on-device only.

We believe in building software that respects user attention and privacy.
